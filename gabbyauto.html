About Us

GABBY AUTOS
Enugu Town, Nigeria

BUILT FROM PASSION,
DRIVEN BY EXCELLENCE

Gabby Autos was born from a simple yet powerful vision — to redefine the car rental experience in Enugu town. What started as a single vehicle operation has grown into one of the most trusted premium car rental services in Eastern Nigeria.

Our founder, Gabriel, recognized a gap in the market: people deserved more than just a car — they deserved an experience. A fleet of meticulously maintained, high-end vehicles paired with a level of customer service that matches the caliber of the machines we offer.

Every vehicle in our collection undergoes rigorous inspection, professional detailing, and performance checks before it reaches your hands. We don't just rent cars — we deliver confidence, luxury, and peace of mind with every key turn.

From executive sedans for corporate engagements to powerful SUVs and luxury buses for events, Gabby Autos ensures that every ride is safe and smooth.
